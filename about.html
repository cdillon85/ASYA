ABOUT US

Welcome!

All Souls Young Adults aspires to be one of the most vibrant and deeply connected young adult groups in New York City.

Affiliated with the Unitarian Church of All Souls in New York City, we welcome newcomers with different backgrounds, perspectives, and sexual orientations.

Our members are of various ages, with most in their 20s or 30s. People come to our group for several reasons: to have fun and make friends, to engage in meaningful discussions, or to deepen their sense of spirituality.
